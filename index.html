About the Practice

Strategic in vision.
Practical in application.

Bearing Tutela is a fractional technology leadership practice built for companies that need senior expertise now—not in months when a long-term search finally concludes.

The "Bearing" in our name is literal. A bearing is both a direction and a support. Most firms offer one or the other: high-level advice that never touches the ground, or hands-on help that lacks a strategic north star. We square that tension by being integrated members of your leadership team.

The challenges that stall a business at the mid-cap stage are usually structural. We apply the operational patterns that separate high-growth firms from those that stall, giving you the clarity needed to scale.
